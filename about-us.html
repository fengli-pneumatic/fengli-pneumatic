  • Company Profile

  • FENGLI is a dedicated pneumatic tool manufacturer and supplier in China, and we mainly provide worldwide customers with various models of pneumatic breaker, rock drill and pneumatic drill rod. These high-quality yet affordable air tools offer ideal solutions for pavement breaking, rock drilling and other applications.

    Inaugurated in 2004 and with a floor space of 14,000 square meters, our company is only 50km from Beijing, and this brings us extra convenience when shipping. Since our foundation, we have been focused on the R&D of new air powered tools, and we have also cooperated with TOKU, Gardner Denver, SULLAIR and other world-class enterprises to share technical innovation. Along with our state-of-the-art production equipment and well-experienced technical staff, our pneumatic breaker and rock drill are highly appreciated by even the most captious clients in Europe and America. For example, our pneumatic breakers, including TPB-40, TPB-60, TPB-90, B87C, B47 and SK10, are considered to be of leading quality around the globe!

  • Development History

  • 2004
    FENGLI was set up in this year. At the beginning, there were about 10 employees, and the factory area was only 3500 square meters.

    2005
    We independently developed a series of pneumatic picks including B87C, B67C and so on. These pick hammers were extensively utilized in mining, road construction, bridge construction and other infrastructure construction projects.

    2006
    After two years of effort, our air tools were highly praised by global clients, leading to a pleasant order boom-fast increase in orders. Given this, we decided to expand our production capacity by introducing several CNC lathes. At that time, the total number of employees reached up to 30.

    2007
    FENGLI gained the import and export trading right, and started to explore overseas market. In the same year, we successfully developed TPB-90, TPB-60, TPB-40 and TCA-7 pneumatic breakers that were comparable with TOKU products.

  • Enterprise Philosophy

  • Customer
    As a customer-oriented pneumatic breaker and rock drill producer in China, FENGLI puts customers in mind all the time, no matter what we are doing. We want to be the leader of any market which we are competing in, so we try to fabricate high quality products and provide all-round services at the same time. We are dedicated to the continuous improvement of product, production process and after-sales service in regards to quality and productivity, thus offering competitive air tools for worldwide customers.

    Staff
    FENGLI takes its employees as the most important asset, and would like to create such a working environment that stimulates innovation, team work, entrepreneurship and success. To achieve this goal, we take a proactive approach to protect the safety and well-being of our staff, making them realize that they are respected and fairly treated. In addition, we also provide an aggressive training program to promote personal development for every employee.

    Safety
    Employees' health and safety deserve our special attention all the time. Hence, we provide our employees with necessary tools to ensure safe working, while safety-related training courses are also offered to help them avoid danger when making pneumatic breakers or rock drills. Moreover, we never stop improving current production process to make the entire work flow as safe as possible.
